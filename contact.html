Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment online through our system by logging in or signing up. Alternatively, you can call us during business hours.

What should I bring?

Just bring your cat! We provide all grooming supplies. If your cat has any special requirements or medical conditions, please let us know in advance.

How long does grooming take?

Grooming time varies by service, typically ranging from 15 minutes for a nail trim to 2 hours for our premium package. We'll give you an estimated time when you book.

Do you accept walk-ins?

We prefer scheduled appointments to ensure the best service, but we may accommodate walk-ins based on availability. Please call ahead to check.
